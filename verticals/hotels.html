Premium hospitality demands premium air. Guests notice — even if they can't articulate why. Great sleep, no musty rooms, no AC smell.

5-star service standards Per-room control 24/7 guest comfort

The problem

What guests actually complain about

Air quality shows up in reviews as 'stuffy rooms', 'AC smell', and 'poor sleep' — it's rarely called out directly.

'Stuffy'

Most common complaint

Guest reviews mention stuffy rooms, musty smells, and AC odors. Root cause: no fresh air.

CO₂ spike

Couples in sealed rooms

Two adults in a sealed hotel room for 8 hours: CO₂ reaches 2,500+ ppm. Poor sleep is inevitable.

Review impact

1 star = 10% revenue

Small air quality complaints cascade into poor reviews, affecting pricing and occupancy directly.

Our solution

Hotel-grade ventilation, invisible to guests

Flush-mounted grilles. Silent operation. Per-room control. Guests feel the difference without seeing anything.

01

Per-room ERV installation

Each guest room gets dedicated ventilation. No shared air between rooms. No cross-contamination.

02

Lobby and restaurant systems

High-exchange rates for public spaces where occupancy varies wildly through the day.

03

Aroma integration option

Signature hotel scents can be delivered through the ventilation system at very low concentrations.

04

Housekeeping reports

Rooms with poor air get flagged. Housekeeping investigates and reports. Proactive quality control.
